Marvelov filmski univerzum (engl. Marvel Cinematic Universe - MCU) je americka filmska fransiza i deljeni univerzum superheroja, zasnovanih na likovima koji se pojavljuju u stripovima kompanije Marvel. Fransiza je prosirena da ukljuci stripove, kratke filmove i televizijske serije. Ostvario je preko 17 milijardi dolara na globalnom nivou sto ga cini najvecom filmskom fransizom svih vremena.

Deljeni Marvelov univerzum je ostvaren preko ukrstanja vise razlicitih prica u jednu zajednicku, kao i preko likova koji se pojavljuju u razlicitim filmovima. Fil Kolson, kojeg glumi Klark Greg, originalni je lik u Marvelovom Filmskom Univerzumu koji se pojavljuje u svim oblicima koji cine fransizu.

Kevin Fajgi je napravio svaki film u Marvelovom Filmskom Univerzumu. Filmove su napisali i rezirali razliciti pojedinci, a u njima se pojavljuje ogromna grupa glumaca. Mnogi glumci, kao sto su Robert Dauni Junior, Kris Evans, Kris Hemsvort, Samjuel L. Dzekson i Skarlet Dzohanson, pod ugovorom su da glume u visebrojnim filmovima.

Negativci
Ultron
Ultron was an artificial intelligence peacekeeping program created by Tony Stark from the decrypted code derived from the Mind Stone encased within Loki's own Scepter, retooled by himself with the help of Bruce Banner with the intent being to protect Earth from any and all domestic and extraterrestrial threats coming within the future.
Loki
Loki Laufeyson was the biological son of King Laufey, the ruler of the Frost Giants in Jotunheim. Shortly after his birth, Loki was then abandoned and left to die. Found by the Asgardian King Odin, he was taken to Asgard and raised by Odin and his wife Frigga as an Asgardian prince, along with their true biological son Thor.When he grew up, he became known as the "God of Mischief".
Erik Killmonger
Erik Stevens, born N'Jadaka, was a former Navy SEAL soldier of Wakandan origin through his father, Prince N'Jobu. His savagery while serving in the US military black-ops unit, earned him the nickname Killmonger.
Thanos
Thanoss a fictional supervillain appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The character, created by writer/artist Jim Starlin, first appeared in The Iron Man #55. Thanos is one of the most powerful villains in the Marvel Universe and has clashed with many heroes including the Avengers, the Guardians of the Galaxy, the Fantastic Four, and the X-Men.
Red Skull
Johann Schmidt is the former head of HYDRA-Abteilung, a special weapons division of the Nazi Schutzstaffel and the modern-day incarnation of the ancient HYDRA society. A brilliant scientist in his own right, he fought his way through the ranks of Nazi elite to become a confidant of Adolf Hitler himself.
Vulture
Adrian Toomes is the former owner of the Toomes Salvage Company, based inside New York City, who chose to be a criminal after he lost his livelihood as Damage Control, a joint venture between the federal government and Tony Stark, was created.
